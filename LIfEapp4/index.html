About EcoWallCheck

EcoWallCheck (Life Cycle Impact Evaluation for Buildings) is a research tool developed for evaluating environmental impacts and thermal performance of building envelope systems.

The database includes comprehensive lifecycle assessment data for construction systems, covering production phase impacts (A1-A3) and thermal performance metrics including U-values and thermal capacity.
